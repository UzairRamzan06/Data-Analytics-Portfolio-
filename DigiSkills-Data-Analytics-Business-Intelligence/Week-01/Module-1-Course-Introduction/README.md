# 📦 Module 1 — Course Introduction & Data Analytics Fundamentals

## 🎯 Overview

This module introduces the foundational concepts of **Data Analytics** and **Business Intelligence**, explaining how data is used in modern organizations to support decision-making, improve performance, and solve business problems.

It also provides a high-level understanding of the course structure, tools, and expected outcomes.

---

## 📚 Lectures Covered

* Lecture 01: What is Data Analytics & Business Intelligence?
* Lecture 02: About Instructor
* Lecture 03: Who Should Enrol and Why it Matters?
* Lecture 04: Common Challenges in Data Analytics Projects
* Lecture 05: Course Roadmap - Tools, Techniques, and Outcomes
* Lecture 06: Overview of Tools for Data Analytics and Business Intelligence

---

## 🧠 Learning Objectives

By completing this module, I will be able to:

* Understand the concept of Data Analytics and Business Intelligence
* Recognize the importance of data in business decision-making
* Identify real-world challenges in analytics projects
* Understand the course roadmap and learning structure
* Become familiar with tools used in the industry
