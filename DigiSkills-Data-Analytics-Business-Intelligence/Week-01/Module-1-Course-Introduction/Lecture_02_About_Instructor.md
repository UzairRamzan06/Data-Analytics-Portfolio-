**Q1. Why is continuous learning important in Data Analytics?**

**Answer:**

Because technologies, business requirements, and analytical tools evolve continuously. Data professionals must regularly update their skills to remain effective and competitive.

**Q2. Why is practical experience important alongside theoretical knowledge?**

**Answer:**

Practical experience helps professionals apply concepts to real business problems, making them more effective in the workplace.

---

## Scenario-Based Question

**Scenario:**

You have completed an online analytics course but have never worked on any practical projects.

**What should you do before applying for Data Analyst roles?**

**Suggested Answer:**

Build portfolio projects, practice using real datasets, document the learning process, publish work on GitHub, and strengthen technical skills through hands-on exercises.
