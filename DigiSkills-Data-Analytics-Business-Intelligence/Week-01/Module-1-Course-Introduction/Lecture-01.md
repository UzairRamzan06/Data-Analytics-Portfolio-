# 📘 Lecture 01 — What is Data Analytics & Business Intelligence?

---

## 🎯 Overview

This lecture introduces the fundamental concepts of **Data Analytics** and **Business Intelligence (BI)**. It explains how raw data is transformed into meaningful insights that help organizations make better business decisions.

---

## 🎯 Learning Objectives

By the end of this lecture, I will be able to:

* Understand what Data Analytics means
* Understand the concept of Business Intelligence
* Identify the difference between data, information, and insights
* Recognize how businesses use data for decision-making

---

## 🧠 Key Concepts

### 📊 Data Analytics

The process of examining raw data to uncover patterns, trends, and insights.

### 📈 Business Intelligence

A combination of strategies, tools, and technologies used to analyze business data and support decision-making.

### 🔄 Data → Information → Insight

Raw Data → Processed Data → Meaningful Insight

---

## 📝 Detailed Explanation

Data Analytics focuses on analyzing historical data to identify trends and patterns. Business Intelligence uses these insights to support strategic business decisions.

For example:

* A retail company analyzes sales data to understand customer buying behavior.
* A bank analyzes transaction data to detect fraud.

---

## 💼 Business Perspective

Organizations use Data Analytics and BI to:

* Improve decision-making
* Increase efficiency
* Reduce costs
* Understand customer behavior
* Gain competitive advantage

---

## 🌍 Real-World Example

A company like Amazon uses data analytics to:

* Recommend products
* Track customer behavior
* Optimize supply chain
* Improve user experience

---

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes

* Confusing Data Analytics with Data Entry
* Thinking BI is just reporting
* Ignoring business context behind data
