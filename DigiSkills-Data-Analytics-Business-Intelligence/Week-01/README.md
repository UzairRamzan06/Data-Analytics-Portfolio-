# 📘 Week 01 — Introduction to Data Analytics & Business Intelligence

## 🎯 Overview

Week 01 focuses on the foundational concepts of **Data Analytics** and **Business Intelligence (BI)**. This week builds the base for understanding how raw data is transformed into meaningful insights that support business decision-making.

This week also introduces the overall workflow of data analytics and how modern organizations use data to improve performance, efficiency, and strategic planning.

---

## 📚 Topics Covered

* Introduction to Data Analytics
* Introduction to Business Intelligence
* Role of Data in Modern Businesses
* Types of Data (Structured vs Unstructured)
* Data-Driven Decision Making
* Basic Analytics Workflow
* Overview of Tools (Excel, SQL, Power BI)

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used

* Microsoft Excel (Introduction)
* Conceptual understanding of SQL & Power BI
* Business Intelligence frameworks
