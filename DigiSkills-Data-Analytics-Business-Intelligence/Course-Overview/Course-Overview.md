# 📘 Course Overview — Data Analytics & Business Intelligence

## 🧭 Introduction

This document provides a detailed breakdown of the **DigiSkills Data Analytics & Business Intelligence course**, including its objectives, learning outcomes, structure, and practical applications.

The purpose of this overview is to serve as a **reference guide** throughout my learning journey and portfolio development.

---

## 🎯 Course Objective

The main objective of this course is to develop practical skills in:

* Data Analysis
* Business Intelligence
* Data Cleaning & Preparation
* Data Modeling
* Dashboard Development
* KPI Reporting
* Data-driven Decision Making

The course focuses on transforming raw data into meaningful insights that support business decisions.

---

## 🧠 What I Will Learn

By the end of this course, I will be able to:

* Analyze data using Excel, SQL, and Power BI
* Clean and prepare raw datasets for analysis
* Build interactive dashboards and reports
* Create KPIs and business performance metrics
* Apply ETL concepts in data workflows
* Understand relational data models
* Extract insights from complex datasets
* Communicate findings effectively
